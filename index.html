About

A little about me

I'm a big data engineer working on streaming systems and the platforms around them. I started out in software development — Java, ETL, complex event processing — and gradually moved deeper into data engineering. These days I split my time between batch and streaming work at telco scale.

I gravitate toward problems where the data is messy, the latency matters, and the answer still has to be right. Production streaming is full of those — and I enjoy chipping away at them.
