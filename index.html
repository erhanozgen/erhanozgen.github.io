About

With five years in big data engineering, I focus on designing and operating data systems that have to keep pace with real volume. Based in Istanbul, I enjoy the architectural side of the work — figuring out how the moving parts fit together at scale.

Whether it's real-time streaming with Spark and Kafka, complex event processing, or batch ETL pipelines, I focus on building infrastructure that's reliable, observable, and pleasant to maintain. I'm curious about where the field is heading, and I try to learn whatever helps me build a little better.
